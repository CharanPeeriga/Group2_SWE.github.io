Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our FAQ section. Here you'll find answers to the most common questions about Event Pulse and how to use our platform.

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Login" button in the navigation bar, then select the "Register" tab. Fill out the required information including your name, email, and password, then click "Register". Once registered, you can immediately start browsing and booking events.

How do I book an event?

To book an event, first browse through our events page to find something that interests you. Click on "View Details" to see more information about the event. If you decide to attend, click the "Book Now" button and follow the instructions to complete your booking. You'll need to be logged in to complete the booking process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. We also offer payment through PayPal for added convenience. All transactions are secure and encrypted to protect your financial information.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend an event?

Refund policies are set by the event organizers and vary by event. Most events offer refunds if canceled within a certain timeframe before the event starts. Check the specific event's details page for refund information. If you need to request a refund, go to your account dashboard and select the event you wish to cancel.

How can I host my own event?

To host an event, you'll need to register for a host account. Once logged in, go to your dashboard and select "Create Event". Fill out the event details including title, description, date, time, location, capacity, and ticket price. Submit your event for review, and once approved, it will appear in our event listings.
